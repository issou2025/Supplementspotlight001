Dymatize ISO100 Hydrolyzed Whey Isolate Review

By Dr. Jane Doe | Last Updated: October 28, 2023

Dymatize ISO100 is a premium protein powder known for its purity and rapid absorption. It's a favorite among serious athletes and those with sensitive stomachs. But is the higher price tag justified? We analyzed its formula and put it to the test.

Ingredient Analysis

The key feature of ISO100 is its use of 100% Hydrolyzed Whey Protein Isolate. This means the protein is broken down into smaller particles for faster digestion and absorption. It's also filtered to remove excess lactose, fat, and carbs, resulting in an incredibly lean profile: 25 grams of protein, 0 grams of fat, and less than 1 gram of sugar per serving.

Effectiveness & Performance

For post-workout recovery, the rapid absorption is a noticeable benefit. It's extremely light on the stomach and mixes instantly, even with a spoon. The Fruity Pebbles flavor is surprisingly accurate and enjoyable, making it a treat to drink. For those on a strict diet or who are lactose intolerant, ISO100 is one of the best options available.

  • Extremely fast-digesting hydrolyzed isolate
  • Virtually zero fat, sugar, or lactose
  • 25g of protein and 5.5g of BCAAs
  • Incredible mixability and unique flavors
  • Perfect for sensitive stomachs
  • Significantly more expensive than whey concentrate
  • Uses artificial sweeteners and flavors
  • Thin consistency may not be satisfying for some

Final Verdict

Dymatize ISO100 is a top-tier protein isolate that delivers on its promises of purity and speed. If your budget allows and you prioritize rapid absorption and low lactose, it is an outstanding choice.

9.5 / 10
