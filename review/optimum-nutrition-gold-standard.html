Optimum Nutrition Gold Standard 100% Whey Review

★★★★★ 5.0
Updated: March 2024

Product Overview

Optimum Nutrition's Gold Standard 100% Whey is arguably the most recognizable protein powder on the planet. For years, it has been a go-to for everyone from professional athletes to casual gym-goers. But does it live up to its legendary status? We took a deep dive to find out.

Advertisement

Ingredients Analysis

The primary ingredient is a protein blend consisting of Whey Protein Isolate, Whey Protein Concentrate, and Whey Peptides. The use of isolate as the main source is a big plus, as it's a purer form of protein with less fat and lactose. It also includes Aminogen and Lactase to aid digestion, which is a thoughtful touch for those sensitive to dairy.

Advertisement

Taste & Mixability

With 24 grams of protein, 5.5 grams of BCAAs, and 4 grams of glutamine per serving, the formula is solid for muscle recovery and growth. In our testing, it mixed easily with both water and milk, with no clumps. It's a reliable workhorse supplement that delivers exactly what you expect: a high-quality protein source to help you meet your daily macros and recover from tough workouts.

Value for Money

    Pros

  • High-quality protein blend (isolate primary)
  • Excellent mixability and taste (Double Rich Chocolate)
  • Includes digestive enzymes
  • Trusted and widely available brand
  • Great value for money

    Cons

  • Contains artificial sweeteners (sucralose)
  • Some flavors can be overly sweet
  • Not the purest isolate on the market
