About Supplement Spotlight

Supplement Spotlight exists to cut through hype and bring you honest, transparent, science-backed supplement information. Our expert team of researchers and trainers reviews every product using rigorous, hands-on, evidence-based methods—so you can make truly informed choices.

Our Mission & Principles

  • Deliver unbiased, research-driven supplement reviews for everyone.
  • Always put science, transparency, and user trust before hype or sponsorships.
  • Update guides and reviews when new research or better products become available.

How We Review

  • Analyze ingredient lists and dosages for scientific validity.
  • Check third-party certifications and batch reports for safety.
  • Test key products hands-on for taste, mixability, and results.
  • Share full pros & cons, with clear affiliate disclosures.
